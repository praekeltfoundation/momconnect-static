THE MOMCONNECT SURVEYS

MomConnect is a South African National Department of Health (NDOH) initiative that and uses mobile technology to improve the health of pregnant women, newborns and infants at national scale. Through the platform, every pregnant woman in South Africa can register to receive free, informative, stage-based messaging for the first year of her baby’s life. MomConnect is an initiative that is run by a consortium of essential partners (“the MomConnect Partners”). Their logos are included below.

MomConnect also provides access to an Electronic Communication Helpdesk and the ability for women to rate the service they receive at the clinic. Ratings and questions are sent directly to the NDOH officials who can respond to queries and ensure that a high level of service is available at every clinic.
